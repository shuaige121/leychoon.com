BCA ATTC

Ley Choon is a BCA Approved Training and Testing Centre (ATTC) incorporated in May 2011. We provide training and tests (skills assessments) for construction workers as a platform to equip them with the knowledge and practical skills to operate the hydraulic excavator and bulldozer machines.

Our experienced trainers are always on hand to address the needs of each and every candidate. With patience and dedication, they will help candidates meet the objectives to achieve good results.

Skill Levels & Programmes Available

1. CoreTrade Skills Assessment

The CoreTrade Scheme was created to develop a pool of competent and experienced tradesmen to raise quality and productivity in the built environment sector.

2. Skills Evaluation Certificate (SEC / SEC(K))

The Skills Evaluation Certificate (Knowledge), or SEC(K), aims to raise skills, productivity, and safety standards in the construction industry. It is one of the most direct routes for upgrading to Higher-Skilled (R1) status under MOM's levy scheme.

3. Accelerated Learning Programme (ALP)

The ALP provides an express route for experienced workers to achieve higher-skilled status through a 1-day training course and a practical skills validation (4‚Äì5 hours) conducted at a BCA ATTC.

Hydraulic Excavator Operation (As Lifting Machine)

Welcome to the Hydraulic Excavator Operations (As Lifting Machine) Training course. This program is conducted by Ley Choon Construction & Engineering Pte Ltd BCA Approved Training and Testing Centre.

Key Learning Outcomes

PS1

Distinguish the various hydraulic excavator models commonly employed in the construction sector.

PS2

Recognize the key elements comprising a hydraulic excavator.

PS3

Describe the purposes of safety mechanisms and indicators installed on the hydraulic excavator.

PS4

Execute the prescribed manual signals and communication methods for operating a hydraulic excavator.

PS5

Adhere to proper operational protocols and safety measures.

PS6

Analyze Lifting Plans and undertake necessary preparations before engaging in lifting activities.

PS7

Review the load indicator monitor and understand warning signs for effective interpretation.

Bulldozer Operation

The Bulldozer Operation course aims to equip trainees with the knowledge and skills required to meet the standards required of the test level.

Course Content

  • General knowledge of the bulldozer
  • Daily inspection and weekly maintenance of the bulldozer
  • Pre-start and shut-down procedures
  • Manoeuvre and park the plant through the bulldozer test circuit
  • Mark out the boundary of the trench according to the drawing
  • Good practices for backfilling, travelling and loading excavated materials onto vehicles
  • Post operation procedure

Construction Plant Operation (non-lifting) CET Online

We are one of the ATTC providing online CET training for Construction Plant Operation (non-lifting).

In order to continuously upgrade the construction workforce to support improvements in productivity, quality and safety, all CoreTrade personnel are required to attend CET courses under their respective registered trade category for CoreTrade renewal.

Course Overview

The CET in Construction Plant Operation Training (non-lifting) is a half-day course covering:

  • Latest codes and regulations
  • Good practices in non-lifting operation
  • New technology in non-lifting machines
  • New hazards in non-lifting operation
  • Latest case studies of lifting accidents

Essential Road Maintenance Training Program

This program is conducted by Ley Choon Construction & Engineering Pte Ltd. The Essential Road Maintenance Training Program is designed to equip participants with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills essential for executing road maintenance works safely and efficiently.

This program blends theoretical instruction with hands-on practice to prepare participants for real-world challenges in road construction and maintenance.

Learning Outcomes

LO1

Comply with LTA Code of Practice for Traffic Control at Work Zones and WSH regulations during road maintenance operations.

LO2

Apply road safety protocols and correctly use Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) to ensure a safe work environment.

LO3

Interpret Traffic Control Plans (TCPs) and implement traffic control measures in compliance with approved procedures.

LO4

Perform pavement maintenance tasks, including defects identification, patching, and resurfacing.

LO5

Operate and maintain road maintenance machinery and equipment safely.

LO6

Apply excavation safety measures and soil stabilization techniques to prevent trench collapse.
